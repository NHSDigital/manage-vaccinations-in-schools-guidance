You can copy and edit the email templates on this page to suit your needs.

You do not have to use them - they’re provided as examples.

## Emails for SAIS teams to send to schools

- [Request class lists from a school](class-list-request.md)
  Help a school send pupil information in the right format for uploading files into Mavis

- [Ask a school to check their details are up to date in Get Information about Schools (GIAS)](check-gias-request.md)
- [Tell schools you’re changing the way you manage consent for vaccinations](changing-consent-process-for-schools.md)
  Let schools know you’ll be using Mavis to contact parents directly for vaccination consent and explain what information they’ll need to share with you in advance
- [Ask a school to request consent for children flagged as sensitive](request-for-schools-sensitive.md)
  Ask a school to edit and send a request to complete the consent form to parents of children with no contact details
  

## Emails for schools to send to parents

- [Tell parents about changes to how consent will be requested](changing-consent-process-for-parents.md)
  Let parents know that future consent requests for school vaccinations will come directly from the NHS, not the school.
- [Ask parents of children flagged as sensitive for consent to vaccinate](consent-email-sensitive.md)
  Ask parents of children with no contact details to complete the consent form
